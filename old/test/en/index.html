Stay in Tuscany

Casa del Lecceto: your holiday in an old house, in the Tuscan countryside near the sea.

The residence Casa del Lecceto is nestled within a nineteenth-century manor house, renovated with Tuscan-style furnishings to create a unique place for dining and relaxation. With three large apartments, one spacious studio apartment and a pool of 128sq.m available to guests, Casa del Lecceto is the ideal place for a stay that combines rest and excursions, sea and hills, comfort and contact with nature. Small farm animals and horses complete the welcoming party for friends who want to spend their holidays in this great country house.

Nearby

Located about 2 km from the village of Venturina Terme, the residence is just a few minutes drive from the Etruscan Coast, with the park and the sea of Rimigliano, the Gulf of Baratti and Populonia, Piombino and the ferries to the island of Elba. In the immediate vicinity are the Baths of Calidario and Valle del Sole. The location of Casa del Lecceto also allows easy access to the main historical and tourist sites of Tuscany, from the nearby villages of Campiglia Marittima and Suvereto to the towns of Volterra, Massa Marittima and San Gimignano and the cities of Pisa, Siena and Florence.
